About Me

Hello! My name is Márcio and I’m from a small town near Braga, Portugal. Since I can remember I’ve had a passion for video games which lead me towards computers and ultimately programming. Nowadays I develop my own video games and I’m also passionate about developing web applications, mainly on the entire frontend spectrum.

I consider myself a well-organized and hardworking person with a creative streak. I'm always keen to learn new technologies that excite me and improve my skills.
